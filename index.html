What We Do

Our Services

From precision railings to certified fire suppression pipelines — we handle every stage of metal fabrication with industrial-grade materials and experienced craftsmen.

Railing Fabrication
Custom steel and stainless railings for staircases, balconies, and commercial facades. Sub-railing and fancy apparel finishes available.
Structural Framing
Heavy-duty 5×11 E-channel framing, structural steel sections, and load-bearing assemblies for industrial and commercial builds.
Fire Pipe Line
Design, installation, and service of fire suppression pipeline systems. Certified equipment supply for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.
Surface Finishing
Sandblasting, Ram-GT-20 graze treatment, and protective coatings to ensure durability and corrosion resistance on all metal surfaces.
Selfy & Safety Service
Inspection, maintenance, and certification of fire safety equipment. Annual AMC contracts available for residential societies and commercial complexes.
Camp Setup & Marking
Complete on-site camp fabrication, structural marking, alignment, and finishing services for large-scale construction and industrial projects.
Who We Are

Built on Precision, Trusted by Industry

Metal Master Fabrication & Fire Service is a Mumbai-based metal engineering firm specialising in structural fabrication and certified fire safety installations. We serve contractors, builders, and industrial clients across Western Mumbai.

Operating from our workshop at Goregaon (E), we combine hands-on craftsmanship with strict quality standards — delivering work that passes inspection and stands for decades.
